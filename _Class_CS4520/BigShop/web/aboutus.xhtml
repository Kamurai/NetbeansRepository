The Sally Shop Company was founded in 2001 by two friends who love exotic animals. We are proud to serve our customers and offer an exceptional level of service. We want to share our passion with everyone that is curious or interested in our lovely animals. If you have any questions, comments, and/or suggestions please don't hesitate to let us know!

We have a wide selection of salamanders, newts, and various neotonic amphibians. Please visit the links at the top of the page to view our selections and to go to the checkout page.
